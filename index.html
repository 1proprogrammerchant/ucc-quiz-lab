Frequently asked questions

Is this free to use?

Yes! UCC Quiz Lab is 100% free and open to all students. No sign-up required.

Can I retake quizzes?

Absolutely. Take any quiz as many times as you want. Your final score is what counts.

How does the scoring system work?

You earn points based on correct answers. Your streak builds a bonus multiplier (up to 2x). Stars and letter grades are calculated automatically.

What topics are covered?

We have 40 questions split between Campus Life (networking, study habits) and CS Basics (algorithms, web dev, databases).

Can I download my scores?

Scores are saved in your browser. Future updates will include export and leaderboard features.

Is my data stored?

All scores are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or transmit personal data.

About this project

UCC Quiz Lab is a playful, high-performance quiz platform built for the University of Commonwealth Caribbean. It combines modern frontend design with a C++ WebAssembly scoring engine for blazing-fast calculations.

The full tech stack includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6 modules), C++/WebAssembly (via Emscripten), Python dev server, and Docker containerization.
