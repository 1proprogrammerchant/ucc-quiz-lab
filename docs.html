Frequently Asked Questions

Can I retake quizzes?

Yes! Take any quiz unlimited times. Your best score and streaks are tracked automatically.

How does the streak system work?

Each correct answer adds to your streak. A wrong answer resets it to 0. Your streak multiplier increases from 1.0x to 2.0x as your streak grows.

Are my scores saved?

Scores are saved in your browser's local storage. They persist even after closing the tab.

What if I get a question wrong?

You'll see the correct answer highlighted in green. Use this to learn and avoid making the same mistake again.

Can I skip questions?

Yes! Click the "Skip" button to move to the next question without answering. Skipped questions count as incorrect for scoring.

How many questions are there?

40 total questions: 20 about Campus Life and 20 about CS Basics. You can filter by track or tackle all of them.
